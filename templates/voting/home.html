Why NigeriaVotes?

How It Works

1

Register with Your NIN/VIN

Provide your voter ID and personal details, then capture 5 facial images from different angles to create your biometric template.

2

Facial Authentication

On election day, enter your voter ID and pass the facial recognition + liveness check — proves you're the registered voter, live in person.

3

Cast Your Vote

Choose your candidate on the digital ballot. The system enforces one-vote-per-voter cryptographically.

4

Receive Your Receipt

Get a unique vote receipt code for personal verification, while full ballot secrecy is maintained.
